**2. What is RPG for Humanity?**  
RPG for Humanity is a cultural-intelligence gaming platform where players learn languages and cultural behavior through immersive missions and emotional micro-scenarios - and creators build worlds, dialogue sets, and cultural knowledge capsules for the entire community. 

M31 is the first living world in this universe.
